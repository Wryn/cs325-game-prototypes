Chicken

  • Originality: I probably don't play enough variety of games, but as of right now I don't know any game of chicken that's similar
  • Technical Merit: Scrolling screen and P2 Physics.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned a little bit of how to use P2 physics.
  • Prototype Assets: I used GIMP and drew all my images. Crash sound from http://soundbible.com/1945-Smashing.html.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Does it count as borrowing from Phaser examples if I only checked to look at the usage of certain functions? If so, I looked at the P2 Polygon example along with the P2 movement example.
  • High Concept: A minimalistic high speed game of Chicken.
  • Theme: Chicken
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard, Achiever
  • Prototype Goal: Scrolling Camera
  • Player Experience Goals: Have players on the edge of their seat as they try their best to score as high as possible without running into the other arrow.
  • Gameplay: The player can speed up by holding the shift key and full stop with the spacebar. There's also a 20 second time limit, so you should try to go relatively fast.
  • Strategies: Manage the speed at a level where you can still track the pointer. Or just go all out and bet on your reaction time.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Can you prove you're not chicken?
  • Target Audience: People who like to test their reaction speed or take risks
  • Play Time: Around 15 seconds.
